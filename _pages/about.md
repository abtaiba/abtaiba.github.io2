Hi, I'm a PhD student at Michigan State University working on Automated Reasoning and Explainability/Interpretability. 

I work on Debugging and Explainability of Formal Methods in the Trustworthy and Reliable Technologies Lab at Michigan State University. 

A lot of my work has intersections with Modal Logic and Formal Verification.

I am also interested in XAI and Mechanistic Interpretability, from an automated reasoning informed perspective, specifically in Formal Methods for Explainable AI. 

If you are interested in any of these, please get in touch, I would love to chat!
